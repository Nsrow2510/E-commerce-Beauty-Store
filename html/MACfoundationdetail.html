M.A.C Studio Fix Fluid SPF 15 is an iconic, high-coverage liquid foundation with a soft matte finish and long-wearing, oil-controlling formula. Step 1: Apply a small amount of foundation to the center of your face.
Step 2: Blend outwards with a brush, sponge, or fingertips to achieve an even, flawless finish.
Step 3: For additional coverage, apply extra product in thin layers as needed.
Step 4: Always shake well before use. Best results when set with powder.
Step 5: Remove with a gentle makeup remover or face wash at the end of the day.
