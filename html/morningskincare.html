Morning Skincare Routine

Start your day with this refreshing skincare routine for glowing, healthy skin:

  1. Cleansser: Begin with a gentle face wash to remove overnight oil and impurities.
  2. Toner: Apply toner with a cotton pad to balance pH and prep your skin.
  3. Serum: Use a Vitamin C serum to brighten and protect from pollutants.
  4. Moisturizer: Hydrate your skin with a lightweight day cream.
  5. Sunscreen: Never skip broad-spectrum SPF to shield from UV rays.
