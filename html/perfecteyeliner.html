How to Perfect the Winged Eyeliner

Follow these simple steps to get sharp, symmetrical wings every time:

  1. Start with a clean eyelid and apply primer or light powder to avoid smudging.
  2. Draw a thin line starting from the inner corner of your lash line.
  3. Use a felt-tip or gel eyeliner to draw a small diagonal flick from the outer corner.
  4. Connect the tip of the wing back to the center of your lid to form a triangle.
  5. Fill in the triangle and smooth out the line.
  6. Repeat on the other eye and clean up edges with concealer if needed.
