How to Find Matching Colour Foundation

Follow these tips to choose the perfect foundation shade for your skin tone:

  1. Identify your undertone: Check if your skin has warm, cool, or neutral undertones by looking at your veins or how gold and silver jewelry look on you.
  2. Test shades on your jawline: Apply foundation shades on your jawline to see which one blends seamlessly with your neck.
  3. Check in natural light: Always check the foundation color in natural daylight to ensure it matches perfectly.
  4. Wait for it to dry: Let the foundation dry down for a few minutes before making your final choice, as it can oxidize.
  5. Get samples if possible: Use sample testers or get small amounts to try before buying full size.
